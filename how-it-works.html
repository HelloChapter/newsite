Get Started

A home renovation timeline that matches your life.

  • Start
    Dreaming

    If you’re reading this, then chances are you’re already dreaming about your apartment renovation. chapter will turn that dream into a reality.

  • Talk to us

    Tell us everything—from what you want your apartment remodel to look like to why you’ve chosen to do it now.

  • Get a real
    estimate

    Chapter takes estimates as seriously as you do. We understand that cost, precise timelines and accurate estimates are key to your decision-making process and to the success of the project.

  • Meet your
    Dream Team

    With chapter, you always work with a team of professionals. Together, we’ll help you plan and design your dream project, and set a budget and timeline that you feel good about.

  • Work with an interior designer

    Your Chapter team includes an experienced and super talented interior designer. Our customers tell us that working with the designer is one of the most exciting parts of the process.

  • Do fun stuff

    Bring your vision to life! Chapter will guide you through the selection process of high-quality materials, while staying within budget, to achieve the luxury design you dreamed of.

  • say “go!”

    Once the final game plan is in place and you give us the green light, then it’s time for the magic to begin. Relax and enjoy the process while we take care of all the work.

  • stay informed

    Chapter’s client portal provides real-time updates throughout the entire project to keep you informed. (And excited!) It’s the ultimate in service with the convenience of technology that we all love.

  • Celebrate

    It’s the moment you’ve been waiting for: your dream apartment renovation is complete! Invite your family and friends, get the bubbly chilled, and plan for lots of selfies.

Making sure every box is ticked. Your personal expert team will adapt to your project’s needs, optimizing communication and anticipating challenges to ultimately deliver your dream home.
