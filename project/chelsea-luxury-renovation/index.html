Chelsea Luxury Apartment Renovation

Chelsea, NYC:

Luxury experience inside trendy Chelsea apartment

Living in the iconic Flower district neighboring the trendy Chelsea area is a dream for many. The Chapter team therefore added some extra special attention when they were approached to gut renovate this two-bedroom two-bathroom apartment.
Inviting in the NYC skyline and its lights was the clients’ main focus when designing their new luxury apartment. The ability to feel the City’s urban beauty was captured by our team through oversized windows stretching as much as five feet by eight feet erasing the boundaries between the indoors and the outside.
