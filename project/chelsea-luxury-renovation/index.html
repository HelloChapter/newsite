Chelsea Luxury Apartment Renovation

225 W 28th St, NYC, NY:

Luxury experience inside trendy Chelsea apartment

For this collaboration with DXA Studio, two-bedroom two-bathroom apartment project located in the Flower District of Manhattan. Small but cozy, this space needed smart layout configuration enhancing its functionality while remaining inviting and warm. The open kitchen makes a play between rough marble stone and soft wood paneling for a peaceful look. While the master bathroom is a black and white graphic delight. DXA Studio conceived the flow through the apartment keeping its owners' needs in mind, making it the perfect NYC pied-à-terre.
