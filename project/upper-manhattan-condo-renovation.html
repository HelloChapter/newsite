Upper Manhattan Condo Renovation

Upper Manhattan, NYC:

Unique mix of modern living and elevated contemporary design

Nestled in Upper Manhattan, this 2-bedroom apartment was another fun project for the Chapter team. The clients were looking to gut renovate their condominium to create a unique space mixing modern living and contemporary designed storage. Bringing as much natural light as possible into the apartment was one of the main priorities when selecting the floor to ceiling windows. The goal was to elevate the space with minimal interference to the layout of the apartment but focusing on quality and rich materials.
