Complete Home And Pool Renovation In The Hamptons

674 Millstone Road, Bridgehampton, NY

Chapter completed an extensive renovation project at 674 Millstone Road in Bridgehampton. The family wanted to upgrade their spacious and well-maintained home to suit their needs and aesthetic preferences better, aiming to rent it out during summer and enjoy it year-round. The renovation encompassed both interior and exterior spaces.

Inside, the kitchen received cosmetic updates. All bathrooms were fully renovated with modern fixtures, tile, accessories, and lighting, while new hardwood flooring was installed throughout the main living areas. A new media room with state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment was created, quickly becoming the family's favorite spot for movie nights and game days.

The exterior work was equally transformative. The construction included a new outdoor patio, a complete pool renovation with a built-in jacuzzi surrounded by bluestone, a wooden deck, and a full exterior kitchen perfect for entertaining. The landscaping was meticulously done creating a peaceful oasis and enhancing the home's curb appeal.
One of the main challenges of the project was coordinating the various elements and trades involved, ensuring that the renovation was completed in a timely manner by the summer and within budget. Effective project management and communication were crucial to meet the client's expectations.

To create a cohesive look throughout the house, the design team, led by Inbar from Indesign Studio, carefully selected finishes and furnishings that harmonized seamlessly. The project increased the value of their home and significantly improved their quality of life. The renovated spaces provided a stunning and functional environment for the family to enjoy and share with guests for years to come.
