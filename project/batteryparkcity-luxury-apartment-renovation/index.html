Battery Park City Luxury Apartment Transformation

280 Rector Pl, NYC, NY:

A breath of new life into Battery Park City Apartment

Situated within the esteemed confines of Battery Park City, this two-bedroom apartment, yearned for a contemporary makeover. The clients, a young couple, desired a more contemporary and open living concept, with a focus on enhancing the kitchen’s functionality by opening up the kitchen to the living room, creating newly updated bathrooms and a spacious master bathroom with a shower conversion.

A beautiful new kitchen island was installed, serving as a central focal point and providing additional countertop space for meal preparation and casual dining. The island incorporated storage options such as cabinets and drawers, ensuring convenience and enhancing the overall efficiency of the kitchen. The open kitchen concept integrates seamlessly with the living room, fostering a harmonious flow between the two spaces.

To enhance the apartment’s overall appeal and ensure long-lasting quality, the outdated and worn flooring was replaced with luxurious vinyl tiles (LVT). This upgrade not only boosts durability but also harmoniously complements the ambiance, creating a modern and seamless aesthetic throughout the living spaces.

Two of the bathrooms underwent a stylish upgrade. For the guest bathroom we used exquisite porcelain tiles that beautifully emulate the luxurious appearance of marble, adding great value to the bathroom. Gray porcelain tiles were installed, accompanied by a mosaic tile shower base, creating a more practical and luxurious space.
