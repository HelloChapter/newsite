Upper East Side Kitchen Renovation

435 E 65th St, NYC, NY:

Farmhouse style and city vibes come together

Before & After

Located on the Upper East Side, this outdated kitchen was ready for a transformation. The clients had a clear vision of their desired outcome, with our skilled interior designer, we successfully brought their dream kitchen to life.

This kitchen stands out with its unique feature of incorporating two quartz stones. The main attraction is the quartz with striking black veins, utilized for the peninsula countertop, waterfall, and even the window seal. Enhancing the overall aesthetic, the flooring has been updated with black and white porcelain tiles with an eye-catching geometric design.

To fulfill the client’s wish for a contemporary farmhouse aesthetic, we selected a wide white farmhouse sink and complemented it with polished nickel fixtures to amplify the farmhouse ambiance.

The seamless integration of numerous custom elements, such as the farmhouse sink, refrigerator door, slim pantry, and full-height backsplash, posed an exciting challenge for our skilled team of workers. However, their dedication and commitment to excellence allowed them to overcome these obstacles, ensuring that every detail was executed with precision.
