UES Townhouse Renovation

Upper East Side, NYC:

Perfect balance of classy and elegant

This remarkable townhouse located in the heart of the Upper East Side was a very interesting project for the Chapter team. For this renovation, our clients wanted to find the perfect balance between maintaining the classic details of the house while marrying them elegantly to contemporary finishes aiming to create a little piece of paradise for themselves. Originally built in 1901, the Chapter team worked on the seven levels which include six stories plus a finished basement, totaling approximately 7,400 square feet. Our focus turned toward preserving the original plaster, moldings and millwork found throughout the house.

The team meticulously sanded down and refinished the century-old hardwood floors running across this mansion. We brought special attention to the overall conservation of the original townhouse’s structure. All bedrooms were renovated with fresh paint and wallpaper.

Perfect balance between maintaining the classic details

Finally, the Chapter team concluded this great renovation with exterior brick pointing which also required scraping and removing of old peeling paint. The exterior was then waterproofed and finished up with a fresh coat of paint in order to bring the final touch to this spectacular townhouse.
