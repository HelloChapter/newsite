Streamlining material selection: How our client portal makes renovations easier

Ira Kugelman
VP Product

At Chapter, we understand that selecting the right materials for your home can be a daunting & stressful task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to make a decision. That’s why we’ve implemented a Material Selection feature in our client portal to make the process easier and more efficient for you.

Streamlining Material Selection

When you log into your personal client portal, you’ll have access the Material Selection feature. Here, you can view the materials offered for your project and make selections at your convenience. You’ll also have access to pictures and descriptions of each option, giving you a clear understanding of what you’re selecting. And if you need to make any changes, it’s easy to do so within the portal.

By using our Material Selection feature, you’ll save time and avoid the hassle of flipping through catalogs or visiting showrooms. Plus, you can communicate directly with your personal interior designer provided by Chapter through the portal, ensuring that you get the results you want.

Material Selection, Chapter Client Portal

With our secure client portal, you can trust that your personal information and project details are protected. And by confirming your material choices quickly, we can start the ordering process faster, which helps ensure a smooth and timely renovation process

If you have any questions or concerns about the Material Selection feature, please don’t hesitate to reach out to our team. We’re here to help you every step of the way.
