Ready to start a new Chapter but have more questions?
We get it.

No fuss, no head aches. The chapter team utilizes technology to simplify processes, milestone tracking and budgeting. All managed by a renovation dream team which includes an interior designer and a project manager dedicating 100 % of their attention to your renovation project. We keep it simple but mostly make it simple for you! Read more about the step-by-step process on our “How it works” page.

Our goal is to deliver a beautiful design, on budget and on schedule. To do so, not only have we developed a digital interface to guide and update you along the way - but we also have a pro-active team composed of a project manager, designer, and a project director to skillfully manage your renovation project. Making sure every box is ticked. Your personal expert team will adapt to your project’s needs, optimizing communication and anticipating challenges to ultimately deliver your dream home.

We are proud to provide complementary interior design advice for each project we manage, thanks to our talented in-house design team. This way we can deliver the quality you deserve while working with professionals who know what can be done (or not!) when renovating your home. No bad surprises, no miscommunication, no going over-budget.

Your main point of contact will be your project manager who will lead the process and coordinate vendors, milestones, and procurement while monitoring your renovation budget.

Your Chapter client portal will be the place to go! We centralize all communications, approvals, materials selection, invoices and progress photos in your digital interface.

You may be dreaming about an apartment remodel, but when it comes to cost, precise timelines and accurate estimates are key to a successful project. Chapter takes estimates as seriously as you do. Our renovation expert will give you a ballpark estimate during your introductory phone consultation. If you like what you hear and want to continue the process, our renovation expert will then meet you at your home for a walk through and collect the full scope of work to prepare the formal work proposal. Precision is key and we like to do things right from the beginning. Because your time is precious.

One of our goals aside from delivering your dream home, is to complete your renovation project as swiftly as possible. Although timelines depend on many factors such as scope of work, permits, approvals and special trade, we plan as much as we can before work starts. Your project manager will have all materials and finishes ordered early enough to manage their timely delivery without getting in the way of the project’s milestones. Covering all grounds and anticipating challenges are key in our timeline building. Our estimations are based on the high number of renovation projects completed over the years and the Chapter team’s construction expertise.
